Films

These are some films I've made, they range from documentary to narrative, experimental, and animation. I love telling stories and enjoy finding new ways to tell them!

reconnect

reconnect is a short animated film that tells the story of a woman in Edmonton, AB who is looking for her first love through a simple phone call. The woman, Deb, discusses her search for her first love, Marshall, and while doing so she also reminisces on her past, and all the things she could have done, should have done, and did not do.

Fer Da Boys

Using DragonFrame, over 4500 frames were shot over the span of a weekend. Countless hours before the shoot were spent printing and cutting each image. This short animation was inspired by the current dominant party culture seen on most university campuses.

Nuts at Queen's

This short documentary film explores the squirrel phenomenon at Queen's University. This project was the first film I was able to direct/film/edit independently, it was also the first time I was able to work with Adobe AfterEffects.

The Trip

The Trip is an experimental film made with 16mm film. Each frame was scratched by hand and a variety of paints and inks were used as well. Due to the fragile nature of the project, the film reel was unfortunately only able to play the film once, luckily, I was able to film the class screening.
